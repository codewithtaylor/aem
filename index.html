About

Aryam's Effectiveness Metric

AEM is a personal evaluation model to measure the effectiveness of any behaviour, habit, or method in daily life.

📐

Suitability

How well the habit or behaviour fits your personality, physical condition, lifestyle, and actual needs. A method that works for others may not suit you at all.

📈

Real Outcome

The actual improvement you noticed after applying the behaviour — not what you hoped for, but what genuinely happened. Honesty here is what makes AEM useful.

🔁

Sustainability

How easy it is to repeat the behaviour without external pressure, burnout, or forcing it. Long-term consistency matters more than short bursts of effort.

➗

The formula

Effectiveness = (Suitability + Real Outcome + Sustainability) ÷ 3. Each pillar is scored 0–100. The result is a single, clear effectiveness percentage.

A note on judgement

AEM is not meant for absolute judgement. It is a tool to help you make realistic decisions based on your own situation and results — not anyone else's standards or expectations.
